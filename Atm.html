Why I love this program

I honestly love this program, it has taught me so much about computers and different programs. Before I went into the ATM program I thought computers were really just for typing things on word and going on the internet, but I learned that computers do so much more. I went from barely learning how to download a picture to coding a website. ATM is a program I will never regret joining, and has taught me skills I will never forget.
