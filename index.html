My Hobbies and Other Interests

I enjoy reading, and creative writing, I also like to play soccer. I started soccer because my best friend Elena plays and after I started I fell in love with it. I've only been playing for about 3 years, but I'm happiest on the soccer field. I mostly play left or right Midfield, or left Forward. I like the position of Midfield because that's where most of the action is so I'm not just sitting and watching from the back of the field.

Another one of the sports I've done is TaeKwonDo. I grew up doing TaeKwonDo, starting at age 4 I didn't know anything but I turned out to be really good at it. When I was 8 years old I got my 1st degree black belt, but I had to leave my studio when I moved from Sacramento to Long Beach. At my new TaeKwonDo studio I became an intern, helping the tiny tigers classes, which were filled with kids ranging ages 2-7. I loved interning at my TaeKwonDo studio because I have a passion for helping people. Whenever they needed extra help I would gladly volunteer, besides they were like family and there's nothing better than spending a little extra time with your family. Even though I ended up quitting when I was 11 I still won't forget the lessons I've learned and the friendships I've built because of TaeKwonDo.
